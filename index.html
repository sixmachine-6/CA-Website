ABOUT US

Welcome to Wissenaire, the annual techno-management fest of IIT Bhubaneswar, renowned as one of the biggest events of its kind in East India. Over three action-packed days, we delve into a diverse range of technological, scientific, and managerial domains, guiding participants through a world of cutting-edge innovations. Each year, WissenAire embraces a new theme to stay at the forefront of advancements.

At Wissenaire, we aim to inspire and ignite the passion for innovation and progress. Our fest serves as a dynamic platform for students to showcase their talents, exchange ideas, and shape the future of technology and management.

Join us at Wissenaire and become part of an exhilarating journey where knowledge meets creativity, and boundaries are pushed. Together, lets explore the fascinating world of transportation and unlock the endless possibilities it holds.

Discover Wissenaire - where innovation takes flight, ideas gain momentum, and dreams find wings!

BENEFITS

Networking Opportunities

Connect with like-minded individuals, industry professionals, and influential leaders.

Skill Development

Gain valuable skills such as leadership, communication, teamwork, and event management.

Swag and Perks

Receive program-branded merchandise, goodies, or discounts on products/services from program partners.

Recognition and Awards

Receive recognition and awards for exceptional performance and achievements during the program.

Events and Conferences

Get invited to attend industry conferences, seminars, and events as a representative of the program.

RESPONSIBILTIES

Promoting the College Fest

Campus ambassadors should actively promote the college fest across various platforms, including social media, college events,and within their respective networks.

Public Speaking

Ambassadors can represent the college fest by delivering speeches or presentations in front of students, faculty, and other stakeholders.

Recruitment

Campus ambassadors can take on the responsibility of recruiting volunteers for the college fest.

Social Media Management

Managing social media accounts dedicated to the college fest is an important responsibility. Ambassadors should regularly update the accounts with event-related news, updates, and promotional materials.

INCENTIVES

Exclusive Merchandise

Offer unique merchandise, such as t-shirts, hoodies, or accessories, branded specifically for campus ambassadors.

Recognition and Certificates

Provide official certificates or letters of recognition to ambassadors for their contribution and achievements.

VIP Access

Grant ambassadors special privileges during the college fest, such as VIP entry to events, backstage access, or priority seating.

Complimentary Passes

Offer complimentary festival passes to ambassadors, allowing them free entry to all events and performances.
